Understanding Gambling & Risk

Many people don’t realize how gambling works — or that the odds are almost always against you. The longer you play, the more you lose.

  • The House Always Wins: Pokies and casinos are designed for profit, not fairness.
  • Randomness Isn’t Luck: Near misses and hot streaks are illusions designed to keep you playing.
  • Stress & Triggers: Gambling to escape boredom, loneliness, or pain can lead to harm fast.

We're here to give you the knowledge and support to make informed choices — without shame.
