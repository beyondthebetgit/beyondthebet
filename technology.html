Each BannedBand includes tamper-detection, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and NFC communication capabilities. When a wearer enters a gambling venue, the venue's alert tablet discreetly detects their presence and displays a silent message to staff.

The band is non-removable without detection or damage and is powered by a secure 9-month battery system. All interaction is passive until triggered, ensuring full privacy and discretion.

The BannedBand ecosystem integrates with a mobile app that supports user control, personal logging, smart notifications, and live help activation.
